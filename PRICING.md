# Systemify Hardware Pricing

## Pricing Strategy

**Mac Minis:** Apple retail price + **$500 markup**  
**Mac Studios:** Apple retail price + **$1,000 markup**

Markup includes:
- OpenClaw pre-installation & configuration
- Skills setup & testing  
- Quality assurance
- Setup documentation
- Standard support
